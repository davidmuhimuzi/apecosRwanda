Hi, We are Apecos Rwanda!

Apecos Rwanda is a non-profit organization that helps children and orphans affected or infected by HIV/AIDS. We aim to make the future brighter for these children and their families by instilling happiness, success and fulfillment in their present.

Apecos Rwanda is dedicated to eradicate poverty of the children who are affected by the HIV/AIDS virus through financial empowerment of their families as well as meeting their educational, clothing, medical, and nutritional needs. We believe that educating young ones will mean a better future for them and their families.
